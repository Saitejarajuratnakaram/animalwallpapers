The Bearded Dragon is a hardy and resilient lizard species native to Australia, known for its distinctive spiny throat, or "beard," which it puffs up when threatened. These reptiles are primarily found in deserts, savannas, and woodlands and are excellent climbers. Bearded Dragons are omnivorous, feeding on insects, plants, and small vertebrates. They are popular pets due to their docile nature and adaptability. In the wild, they thrive in hot, arid environments and are often seen basking on rocks or tree branches.

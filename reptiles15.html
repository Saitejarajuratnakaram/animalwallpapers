The Box turtle, a well-known terrestrial turtle, is recognized for its distinctive hinged shell that allows it to retract completely into its shell for protection. Found in forests, grasslands, and wetlands, these turtles are essential for their ecosystem as they help control insect populations and disperse seeds.
